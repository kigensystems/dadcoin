Required Sound Files for Grill Master Challenge
================================================

Please add these sound files to the /public/sounds/ directory:

1. sizzle.mp3
   - Description: Continuous meat sizzling sound
   - Duration: 3-5 seconds (loopable)
   - Usage: Plays when food is cooking on the grill

2. flip.mp3
   - Description: Spatula flipping/clicking sound
   - Duration: 0.5-1 second
   - Usage: Plays when player clicks on food

3. swat.mp3
   - Description: Quick swat/whack sound
   - Duration: 0.3-0.5 seconds
   - Usage: Plays when player clicks on a fly

4. success.mp3
   - Description: Pleasant "ding" or chime sound
   - Duration: 1-2 seconds
   - Usage: Plays when food is flipped at perfect timing

5. burn.mp3
   - Description: Food burning/charring sound
   - Duration: 1-2 seconds
   - Usage: Plays when food becomes burnt

6. background-bbq.mp3
   - Description: Ambient BBQ atmosphere (distant chatter, birds, etc.)
   - Duration: 30-60 seconds (loopable)
   - Usage: Background ambience during gameplay
